First,heat the bun in gas.
After,removing the bread from gas burner cut some cabbage,Tomatoes Muashroom and other vegetables and kept it on the side of the table.
Then first add a layer of cabbage in the top of one bun.
Add tomatoes after that up of the cabbage layes.
Then,add a pice of cooked meat at the top of tomatoes layer.
Then add 2tablespoons of melted cheese at the top of meat layer to make more delicious.
Then add some mushrooms at the top of cheese layer.
Then add a mayonnaise coating at the top of mushrooms.
After add other vegetable like cocumber of your choise.
Then use some cutting onions an make a layer at the top most end.
Final step, use another pice of bun and place it on the top and apply pressure you can also add sauce in the buns or inside of the vegetables layers between.
Your burger is ready you can now enjoy the burger with coke and frenchfries.
